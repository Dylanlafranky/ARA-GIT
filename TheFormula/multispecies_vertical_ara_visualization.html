4 · The takeaway

Four mammals at different metabolic scales — mouse (521 bpm) up to human (79 bpm) — all show the same local cycle shape after time-rescaling by their own heart period.

Mean correlation across all six species pairs within ±2 rungs of peak: +0.955. Within ±5 rungs: +0.93. The framework's vertical-ARA prediction held without tuning, on independently collected datasets, with strict-causal filtering.

This extends the prior cross-domain result (ECG ↔ ENSO match across 38 orders of φ in time) into biology. Mammalian physiology shares a single shape across the part of the φ-ladder we tested. Next step: corvids and parrots, when we can get clean continuous HRV recordings.
