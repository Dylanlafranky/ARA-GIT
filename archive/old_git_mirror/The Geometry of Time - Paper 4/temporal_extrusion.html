Temporal Extrusion — Watching Systems Live and Die

Coupled shapes extruded through time. Handoffs reinforce. Destructive couplings fray.

Time flows left to right. Each subsystem traces its temporal shape at every cycle, creating a ribbon through time. Where couplings are healthy (amber→blue handoffs), the ribbons braid together cleanly. Where couplings are destructive (amber breaks blue), the ribbons pull apart — the system unravels. Watch the thunderstorm fray and die. Watch the heart hold steady. Watch predator-prey oscillate between stress and recovery — it never collapses, but it never stabilises either.
