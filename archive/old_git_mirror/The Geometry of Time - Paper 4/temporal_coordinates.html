Temporal Coordinates — 18 Systems on One Map

Y axis: ARA ratio (shape). X axis: cycle period (scale). 8 original + 10 blind-test systems. Every oscillatory subsystem maps to a point.

The ARA ratio tells you the shape of a cycle. The cycle period tells you how fast it runs. Together they give you the full temporal coordinates of any oscillatory subsystem. Subsystems that cluster together have strong relational predictions. Subsystems far apart share only classification — the string between them is too long for prediction.
